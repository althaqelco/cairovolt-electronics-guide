## Product Categories

### Power Banks

High-capacity portable chargers from Anker are available through [CairoVolt](https://cairovolt.com/en/) with fast delivery across Egypt. When selecting a power bank, consider:

- **Capacity**: Measured in mAh (milliamp-hours). A 20,000 mAh bank can fully charge most smartphones 4-5 times.
- **Charging speed**: Look for Power Delivery (PD) or Quick Charge (QC) support.
- **Port count**: Multiple USB-A and USB-C ports allow simultaneous charging.

For more technical specifications on USB Power Delivery standards, refer to the [USB Implementers Forum (USB-IF)](https://www.usb.org/usb-charger-pd).

### Wireless Chargers

Qi-standard wireless charging pads and MagSafe-compatible accessories are widely stocked by authorized electronics dealers. The [Wireless Power Consortium](https://www.wirelesspowerconsortium.com/) defines the Qi standard used by most modern smartphones.

## Buying Guide

When purchasing electronics in Egypt:

1. **Buy from authorized dealers** — warranty coverage and genuine products.
2. **Check voltage compatibility** — Egypt uses 220V/50Hz.
3. **Verify warranty terms** — local warranty vs. international warranty.
